Workflows

Real-world patterns for automating your life with email commands.

Quick Capture

Capture ideas instantly

No app switching. Just email yourself from anywhere.

To: you@agentmail.to
Subject: /note Idea for the project
Could use a different approach with Clawederous...

Saved to your knowledge base, searchable later.

Meeting Notes

Log meetings instantly

Send a quick note right after a meeting.

To: you@agentmail.to
Subject: /note Meeting with Gary
Key takeaways: - Timeline confirmed - Budget approved - Next steps: Sarah to draft proposal
Pro tip: Use consistent subject prefixes like "/note Meeting:" for easy filtering.

Daily Journal

End-of-day reflection

Quick log of wins, learnings, and next-day plans.

To: you@agentmail.to
Subject: /log
Wins: Shipped Clawederous homepage Learnings: Typography matters more than I thought Tomorrow: API docs and workflow examples

Reminders

Set reminders via email

Natural language reminders for follow-ups.

To: you@agentmail.to
Subject: /remind tomorrow 9am Email Sarah about the timeline
Need to confirm the milestone dates.

Team Updates

Forward to team channels

Use /claris to route important updates.

To: you@agentmail.to
Subject: /claris Weekly update
Here's this week's progress on the Clawederous project...

Automatically routed to your designated recipient (e.g., Claris).

Thread Summarization

Get AI summaries of email threads

Reply to any thread with /summarize for a quick recap.

To: you@agentmail.to
Subject: /summarize
Brief me on this thread's key points.
